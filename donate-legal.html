Help Us Achieve 501(c)(3) Status

Achieving 501(c)(3) non-profit status is the single most important step to ensure the long-term success and legitimacy of GrinBound. This official designation makes all future donations tax-deductible for our supporters and opens up opportunities for grants and corporate partnerships.

The funds raised on this page are dedicated exclusively to paying for the required 501(c)(3) application fees and the associated legal costs for professional review.
